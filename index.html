White wines: contain little or no red pigmentation. Produced from green or sometimes black grapes. Types of white wine are Riesling, Gewurztraminer, Chardonnay and Sauvignon Blanc.

Red Wines: produced from black grapes and have a red or blue tint. Types of red wine are Syrah, Merlot, Cabernet Sauvignon and Pinot Noir.

Rose Wines: made from black grapes by removing the skins before they deeply color the wine.

Sparkling wines: made from any variety of grapes. Contain carbon dioxide bubbles. Sparkling can be red, white or rose.
